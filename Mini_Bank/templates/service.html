What We Offer

Account Creation Request
click to create

Visitors can fill out a detailed form to request the creation of a bank account. This request is later reviewed by our manager or bank staff.

Account Approval System

Upon submission, your account application is processed and either approved or rejected by the manager. Approved users receive an account number via email.

Secure Login

Once approved, users can securely log in using their unique account number and access banking services.

Fund Transfer

Users can conveniently transfer money to other accounts. Both the sender and receiver are notified via automated email upon successful transactions.

Employee Credit & Debit Access

Bank employees are granted access to manually credit or debit funds into a customer's account for administrative or operational purposes.

Instant Email Notifications

Whether it's a new account approval or a completed transaction, our system ensures prompt email alerts to keep users informed in real-time.
